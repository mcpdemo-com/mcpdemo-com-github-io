What is MCP?

The Protocol

MCP is a standard that lets AI models like Claude connect to external tools and data sources in real time. It's an open specification that defines how AI assistants can securely interact with your applications, databases, and APIs.

The Connection

Instead of hardcoded integrations, MCP creates a universal bridge between AI and the apps you already use. This means Claude can access your calendar, email, project management tools, and more through a single, standardized interface.

The Power

When Claude can access your calendar, email, databases, and APIs, it stops being a chatbot and starts being a true assistant. MCP transforms AI from a conversational partner into an actionable tool that can actually get things done for you.
